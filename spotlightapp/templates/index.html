About Spotlight

Social media has a vast user base, with over 1.5 billion active users per month on Facebook, 400 million active users per month on Instagram, and over 320 million active users per month on Twitter. Celebrities often have profiles on each of these sites. Spotlight is an application that allows you to view your favorite celebrities' social media profiles all in one place: we make it easy for YOU to learn about your favorite celebrities!
